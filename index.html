BIOGRAPHY

I was born on April 29, 2002 in the small town of Goražde, Bosnia and Herzegovina. I started my music career when I was only 4 years old, appearing at a local music competition. After a few years, I applied to the regional music competition for young people "Neki Novi Klinci" and won the first season. Then I participated in the biggest music competition in the Balkans "Zvezde Granda" and won in the first appearance. I currently have 8 recorded songs with over 100 million listens. I started my collaboration with Maybelline in 2023 and since then I have enjoyed every moment together.
